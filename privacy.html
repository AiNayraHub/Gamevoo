Privacy Policy & Data Protection

01 Information Collection

We collect limited personal information such as your Email Address, Username, and unique Device ID. This data is essential for maintaining your account security and synchronizing your earned rewards across sessions.

02 Data Usage

Your data is strictly used to process withdrawals, prevent fraudulent activities, and provide personalized offers via our integrated offerwall partners. We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketers.

03 Third-Party Partners

Gamevoo integrates with third-party advertising and offerwall networks (e.g., CPAGrip, Monlix). These partners may collect device-specific information when you interact with their tasks to verify completion and issue rewards.

04 Security Standards

We utilize high-level encryption and secure database management through Supabase to ensure that your coin balance and profile information remain protected from unauthorized access.
